# Initial Concept
A modern, full-stack Point of Sale (POS) and billing application designed for seamless retail management.

## Target Audience
Retailers, shop owners, and small to medium businesses who need an intuitive, fast, and local billing system with GST/tax integration.

## Key Features
- **Dashboard Analytics**: Real-time insights into sales, product performance, and stock levels.
- **Product Management**: Inventory tracking, custom GST rates, HSN codes.
- **Customer Directory**: Client database with GSTIN tracking and lifetime value metrics.
- **Invoice Generation**: B2B/B2C invoicing with automatic tax splitting, discounts, and payment tracking.

## Goals
Provide a highly responsive, easily deployable, and robust local POS solution that simplifies daily retail operations.
